Welcome to the John W. Gardner Center (JGC) Network for After School project! This site was created in collaboration with a Stanford University Computer Science class (CS210) and is a work in progress that needs your input!

The goal of this project: To sustain and improve out-of-school-time programs for underserved youth by facilitating collaboration and resource sharing between the youth, adults, and organizations that operate them. While many areas of the site are still under construction we invite you to explore and provide us with feedback Specific areas that are ready for users to use are After School Activities, Program Director, Organizations and Search
